# Title: OAKLAND, CALIF. Charles B. Campbell, Washington, D.C., G-Man, is shown demonstrating the use of sub-machine guns to local police at the target range here. This picture, showing Campbell’s accuracy on the target body, was taken with flash bulb lighting in a fraction of a second, yet eight bullet traces are visible, all striking the figure in the chest and stomach
